HOW TO PLAY

Guess the word in 6 tries. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

If you want to ruin the fun for your friends, add their name and number and once you've solved today's word we will alert them of the word and that you've beaten them to it!

Struggling? Try the hint button!
